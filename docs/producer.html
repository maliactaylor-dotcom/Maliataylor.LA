Producer

As a Producer, Malia loves collaborating creatively to find solutions to the never-ending array of opportunities that arise everyday on sets large and small. Her years as a scrappy, single, mom of four kids tirelessly making magic happen wearing all the hats fueled by love, grit and grace are what forged in her this drive and determination. She came to believe that no matter the circumstance she found her self in, she would have faith that everything would be alright as she did everything in her power to provide the best she could to allow her children to pursue their passions in the arts. Afterall, as J.M. Barre said, "All the world is made of faith and trust and pixie dust." Isn't that how movies are made?
