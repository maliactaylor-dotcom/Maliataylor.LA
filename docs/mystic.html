Mystic

Since her early childhood Malia has had mystical experiences with the Divine. She has spent much of her life developing her inner life to learn and understand the meaning and significance of these experiences, how they have transformed her own personal story, and the impact that has had on her story among the greater human collective. Malia believes that we as humans are the bridge between the natural and the super natural world and those who are open to receiving messages can, at times, and by grace, communicate between the realms. In many ways, her healing practice, as well as her writing and work within the arts, is a mystical practice, all stemming from the artistic "flow state" where the Divine dances.
