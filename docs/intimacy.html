Intimacy Coordinator

As an Intimacy Coordinator, I help curate the physical storytelling of intimate scenes in Film & Television collaborating with the the creative team to bring the vision to life in a safe and protected environment that fosters artistic freedom and curiosity. I do not shy away from challenging stories as my passion and training is in trauma-informed and transformational storytelling. I always look for ways to bring excellence and joy to the work, while delivering effectively with skill and a touch of human kindness.
