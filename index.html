Statements producer • writer • intimacy
Producerhow she leads

Producing is devotion: to the story, to the crew, and to the unseen emotional weather on set. I build environments where excellence doesn’t require harm. We make beautiful things without breaking people.

Writerwhy she writes

Writing is a ritual of listening. I’m drawn to women who refuse simplification, to love that isn’t decorative, and to endings that feel like truth instead of closure.

Intimacy work is power work. I treat consent as choreography: specific, repeatable, adjustable. When boundaries are clear, performance becomes fearless.

Mystic • Healer • Rebelthe through-line
